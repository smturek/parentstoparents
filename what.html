What Can I Do?

There are many ways you can help!

  1. Write up stories and advice from your personal experience and send them to us.  We’ll add them to the webpage.  This can be from parents, professionals, anorexics and recovered anorexics.
  2. You can donate to Parents-to-Parents for future documentaries.  You can even choose which documentary you would like to contribute to.  We have two documentaries we are hoping to make by the end of 2016.    The first one is:  Body Image: Parents raising children with a healthy body image.  The second one is Anxiety:  Parents teaching children how to alleviate and talk back to anxiety.  As of making this webpage public, we are completely self-funded.
  3. You can share this webpage with others.
  4. If you find a great new book or webpage, let us know and we’ll put it in our resource tab.
  5. If you would like to share experiences in future documentaries, let us know!  We’d love to include as many diverse experiences as possible.  Contact us about what you would like to do and we’ll see what we can do to include you.
